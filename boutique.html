Boutique Hotel

Integrating with heating, ventilation and air conditioning（HVAC) system，local history and special structure, to find unique features to attract tourists

History Introduction

This site has a close relationship with trains. After railroad establishment in 1900s, this area was populated and attracted plenty of people to live here due to the convenience and opportunities brought by the train.

Transportation and View

The west side of the site is a main road, so the entrance should be arranged on west side, or on south side to avoid traffic jam. The view on south side is the best, and the second best is west side. Therefore, main elevation should be put on these two sides.

Concept Formation

The idea based on the shape of the train and the direction of the railroad. Guestroom floors are designed similar like train carriage. These bars either parallel or perpendicular to the direction of the railroad.
